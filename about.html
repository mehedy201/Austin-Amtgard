About Amtgard

What is Amtgard?

Amtgard is a live-action medieval fantasy game that combines foam combat, role-play, and arts and sciences activities. It includes fighting, crafting, performance, service, and community.

What is Nocturnus

Nocturnus is the local park serving the Austin area. It is a weekly battlegame and role-play park that emphasizes teamwork, safety, and sportsmanship.

Culture / expectations

New and experienced players are welcome. A respectful attitude is expected. Garb and class gear are encouraged, but not required for a first visit. The park values teamwork, safety, and community participation.
