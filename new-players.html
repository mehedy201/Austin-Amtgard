Welcome to Nocturnus, the Austin Amtgard park.

Learn how to get started in Amtgard and join the fun. No experience is needed — we’ll help you jump in at your own pace.

What to Expect / What to Bring

  • The best way to begin is simple — show up.
  • Bring water, wear comfortable athletic clothing, and come ready to participate.
  • You do not need a costume to start.
  • We provide loaner gear, explain the rules, and get you involved right away.
  • No experience is required.
  • Recommended bring/wear list: athletic clothing, closed-toe shoes, water.

Garb (Clothing & Costuming)

Garb is your in-game clothing. Making your own is often cheaper, more customizable, and a good way to get involved with the community. Many players are happy to help new people get started.

Learn / DIY Making your own garb is one of the easiest ways to start. Simple outfits can be made with basic materials and minimal cost.

Equipment (Weapons & Gear)

Amtgard uses safe, foam-padded weapons and equipment. You can borrow gear when you start, but many players eventually build their own. Making equipment is cost-effective and a core part of the hobby, and experienced players are usually willing to help.

Building your own equipment is a great way to learn the game and save money. Start simple, follow approved construction guidelines, and ask experienced players for help.

Resource link: Amtgard Wiki Tutorial Collection — https://wiki.amtgard.com/Tutorials

Safety line:All weapons and equipment must pass safety inspection before use on the field.
